SEPTURE

what is septure?

Septure helps you keeping track of all the series you've ever watched. Just like the title of the app suggests, it captures your series. With Septure you'll never lose track of those awesome series you've watched but probably would forget about without this app.

Features

OVERVIEW

Overview of all the series you've watched including their seasons and episodes

ADD

Easily add seasons and episodes to your watched list with autocompleted searching

NOTIFICATION

Reminds you when a new season of your series is going to be aired

SEPTURE

Work in progress
